HRCLUB (The Club of Human Resource Managers)

HRCLUB is a Tianjin-based platform specializing in human resources, with numerous member enterprises coming from Beijing, Tianjin and Hebei Province respectively. Established in Tianjin, it had attracted thousands of companies as members, and gathered more than ten thousand human resource managers and enterprise senior managers by 2015. Annually, HRCLUB organizes over one hundred member activities of all sorts. As the most influential regional professional platform of human resources in China, HRCLUB has its subordinate organs including Easy Employment, Easy Legislation, Training Camp with Simulative Exercises, Remuneration Survey, Elite Magazine and Peer Advisory Group in Human Resources, all of which become excellent examples in the industry.

HRCLUB offers its members messages and information of all kinds, professional advices and data, as well as opportunities for all-rounded professional development. At the same time, it also serves as a platform for the share of industrial practices and network communications. Through professional training, study and discussion on management of human resources, HRCLUB aims at promoting the general development of human resources management level, and making contributions to build prosperous labor relations.

As for Sino-Credit, it shall offer thirty minutes of hotline legal services, free of charge, to the member enterprises of HRCLUB. Sino-Credit is ready to provide specialized legal services for member companies in accordance with their requirements.
