From 1994 to 2004, Mr. Xu served as the HR director and legal consultant in Coca-Cola (Tianjin), and accumulated abundant practical experience on enterprise legal affairs and human resources management of well-known multinational companies.

In 2005, Mr. Xu served as the manager of Senior Employee Relations in Motorola (China), and had an overall responsibility for the management of employee relations.

In 2006, Mr. Xu was appointed the manager of Robertson Stromberg Pedersen (China), mainly providing legal services on China-related import and export trade, investment affairs between China and Canada, business immigration and provincial nominee immigration.

Mr. Xu had long been working as the senior partner and HR director in Tianjin Winners Law Firm before he joined into Sino-Credit. He has served numerous well-known companies in various industries and government institutions, including Scope International (China) Co., Ltd., General (Tianjin) Alloy Products Co., Ltd, Novo Nordisk (China) Pharmaceuticals Co., Ltd., Boeing Tianjin Composites Co., Ltd., Rohm Semiconductor (China) Co., Ltd., Vestas Wind Technology (China) Co., Ltd., Fiberweb (China) Airlaid Co., Ltd., Tianjin Municipal Bureau of Sports, Tianjin Human Resources and Social Security Bureau, TEDA Human Resources and Social Security Bureau.

With long-time experience within the lawyers’ industry, Mr. Xu was elected as the Executive Chairman of HRCLUB, Expert of Business Association in labor relations, Observer of TEDA Chamber of Commerce in labor relations, Campus Tutor of Business School in Nankai University, Part-time Mentor of Management and Economics School in Tianjin University, Instructor of Practice Course of TUFE Business School, Executive Director of Sports Law Branch of Tianjin Law Society, etc. 

Mr. Xu is one of the few experts with well-known multinational human resources management and practical legal counsel experience. As the writer of "Thoughts on A Checkered Career", his book once was on list of books for recommendation in fields of human resources in 2005, which had been highly praised by other HR practitioners. 

As the pioneer, Mr. Xu and some of the outstanding domestic law firms co-founded China's first cross-regional labor law firm alliance in 2013 – High-quality Clients of Sino-Credit, to provide legal services across the region.
