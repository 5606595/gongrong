Ms. Chen Si        Head of Human Resources and Labor Law Department of Sino-Credit

Ms. Chen Si

Tel : +86 22 60336055

E-mail : cs@tjgrlaw.com

Ms. Chen Si graduated from the Law School of Nankai University in 2009 with a bachelor degree in law. After graduation, she worked to intercede labor disputes in Labor Disputes Mediation Board of Tianjin Port Economic Zone. During that period, her abilities in communications and dealing with emergent cases were improved. In 2012, she was employed by a large law firm and began to professionally take charge of legal affairs in areas of commercial operation and human resources management.

When she worked to mediate disputes, Ms. Chen Si dealt with thousands of labor disputes and had gained rich experience accordingly. Since she took the job as a lawyer, she has been concentrating on practicing and studying laws related to human resources and labor law. She has provided consultancy services in cooperation with the labor union for hundreds of companies and employees in Tianjin Port Economic Zone. She once worked as a member of secretaries to help establishing the first non-governmental labor and personnel dispute mediation center in Tianjin. At the same time, she also serves many well-known companies, including Scope International (China) Co., Ltd., Tianjin TEDA HR Services Co., Ltd., Gaize（Tianjin）Industry Co. Ltd., General (Tianjin) Alloy Products Co. Ltd., Novo Nordisk（China）Pharmaceuticals Co. Ltd., Joy Global (Tianjin) Mining Equipment Co., Ltd., Aili（Tianjin）Aluminum Co. Ltd., Tianjin Municipal Bureau of Sports, Boeing Tianjin Composites Co., Ltd. and so on.
