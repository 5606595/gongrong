Practices

Foreign Corporate Legal Services

Basing on the existing industrial characteristics, management framework and corporate culture of our clients, a specific plan for interior compliance auditing shall be tailored by us respectively. In the meantime, we shall also provide meticulous and professional legal counsel services in all areas, such as anti-commercial bribery, governmental administrative licensing, environmental protection, intellectual property, commercial secret protection and so on, to ensure prompt settlement of legal risks incurred during process of operating management.

Our services include:

  • Designation of commercial secrets protection system; protection of intellectual property rights and litigation
  • Company compliance review; providing legal advice on government procurement, and government approval
  • Anti-commercial bribery investigations and compliance review
  • Legal affairs with respect to foreign reorganization, dissolution and liquidation
