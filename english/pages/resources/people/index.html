Practices

As a law firm committed to provide a full range of high-end civil and commercial legal services, our legal team of senior experts has provided legal services for numerous domestic and foreign clients in various professional fields. When our clients need a comprehensive legal service related to different areas, our team of experts are always ready to provide support and close collaboration.

Human Resources and Labor Law Affairs

We have a group of first-class and professional lawyers specialized in Human Resources and Labor Law, especially in affairs related to foreign-invested enterprises, large state-owned and private enterprises. Moreover, we have achieved a large number of success cases and accumulated mature experience in areas such as labor law affairs involved in complex commercial transactions, labor crisis issues occurred during corporate operation and management, as well as large layoff projects.

In dealing with labor law affairs, we never forget our social responsibilities of maintaining harmonious labor relations. We work elaborately to avoid or resolve labor disputes and see it as our primary working objectives. Through the compliance review of human resources management, beforehand counseling and guidance of handling labor disputes, and resolving labor disputes according to laws, we put our best efforts to ensure the maximization of the overall interests of our clients.

Our services include:

  • Compliance Review of enterprise employment and labor relations
  • Risk Assessment of Employee Relations
  • Formulation and review of employee manuals and corporate internal regulations
  • Consultation service with respect to labor protection, work-related injury, social security and occupational diseases
  • The transfer, dissolution and resettlement of employees during process of corporate restructuring, merger and acquisition
  • Layoff project planning and implementation
  • Crisis handling in labor disputes and group/individual labor conflicts
  • Labor arbitration and litigation
  • Policy illustration and legal training
  • Employee background checks
  • Expatriate labor relations management
  • Program design for equity incentive and long-term incentive
  • Collective wage negotiations and democratic management consultation
  • Commercial bribery investigation
  • Disputes between sports clubs and professional athletes
  • Public institution personnel disputes
  • Athlete personnel disputes
