Practices

As a law firm committed to provide a full range of high-end civil and commercial legal services, our legal team of senior experts has provided legal services for numerous domestic and foreign clients in various professional fields. When our clients need a comprehensive legal service related to different areas, our team of experts is always ready to provide support and close collaboration.

Commercial and Corporate Legal Services

Sino-Credit is dedicated to help clients realize fast development and the maximum of commercial interests on the premise of legal compliance. Our strong team of senior lawyers specializing in Commercial and Corporate Affairs shall provide professional and commercial opinions for clients in services of Legal Counsel, Commercial Operation Risk Consultancy, Commercial Litigation Whole-progress Consultation and Agency, etc. with a view to reduce risks associated with transactions for clients.

Our services include:

  • Legal opinions for dispute resolutions with respect to problems during process of corporate foundation, merger and acquisition, operation and dissolution
  • Providing consultation services concerning daily operation legal affairs, including contract review, business negotiations and legal opinions
  • Providing legal opinions on investigation and solution in relation to commercial litigation
  • Providing agency services in litigation and arbitration
